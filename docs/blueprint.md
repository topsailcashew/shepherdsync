# **App Name**: ShepherdSync

## Core Features:

- Member Management: Complete CRUD operations for member records, mirroring the functionality of the Shepherd backend.
- Engagement Tracking: Monitor member involvement across church activities and display the insights in interactive dashboards.
- Ministry Coordination: Manage groups, serving teams, and ministry programs within the church. Visualize and arrange ministry program coordination effectively.
- Family Relationships Visualization: Track households and family connections. Use generative AI tool to identify disconnected members and suggest potential connect groups to administrative staff
- Covenant Partnership Tracking: Track member commitments and spiritual growth milestones to provide valuable information for pastoral staff to manage the church.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#46878D) to convey trustworthiness and spirituality.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated teal (#E0F4F1).
- Accent color: Warm gold (#D4AF37) to highlight important actions and data.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif with high contrast lines to invoke a feeling of elegance; body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for longer text.
- Use clean, modern icons representing key actions and data points, aligning with a user-friendly pastoral care focus.
- Emphasize a clean, card-based layout with intuitive navigation, ensuring easy access to critical member data and ministry management features.
- Implement subtle transitions and animations to guide the user through tasks, like revealing detailed information or confirming actions.
